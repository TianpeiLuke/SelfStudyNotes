## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Harmonic Function

![[Laplace Equation and Poisson Equation#^82f52a]]



>[!important] Definition
>A $\mathcal{C}^2$ function $u: \bar{U} \to \mathbb{R}$ satisfies the *Laplace equation*
>$$
>\begin{align*}
> \Delta u & = 0
>\end{align*}
>$$
>is called a **harmonic function**.
