## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Almost Trivial sigma Algebra


>[!important] Definition
>For a $\sigma$-algebra $\mathscr{F}$, if 
>$$
> \begin{align*}
> \mathcal{P}(A) \in \set{0, 1}, \quad \forall A\in \mathscr{F}
> \end{align*}
>$$ 
>  then $\mathscr{F}$ is **almost trivial**.
