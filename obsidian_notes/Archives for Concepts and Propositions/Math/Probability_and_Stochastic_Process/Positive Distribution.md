## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Positive Distribution

>[!important] Definition
>Let $(\Omega, \mathscr{F})$ be measurable space.
>
>A distribution $\mathcal{P}$ is **positive** if $$\mathcal{P}(\alpha) >0, \quad \forall \, \alpha \neq \emptyset \land \alpha \in \mathscr{F}.$$
