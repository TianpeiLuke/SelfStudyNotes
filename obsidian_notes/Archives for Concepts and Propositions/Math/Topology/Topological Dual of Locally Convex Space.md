## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Topological Dual of Locally Convex Space

>[!important] Definition
>Let $X$ be a **locally convex space**.
>
>The space of all *continuous linear functionals* on $X$ is called the **topological dual** $X^*$.
