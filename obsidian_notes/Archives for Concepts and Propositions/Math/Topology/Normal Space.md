## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**:  Normal Space $T_4$


>[!important] Definition
>The space $X$ is said to be **normal ($T_4$)** if for each pair $A$, $B$ of _disjoint **closed sets**_ of $X$, there exist *disjoint open sets* containing $A$ and $B$, respectively.
