## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Retraction Map

>[!important] Definition
>A **retraction** of a *topological space* $X$ onto a *subspace* $M \subset X$ is a *continuous map* $$r: U \to M$$ such that the *restriction* $r|_{M}$ is the *identity map* of $M$.
