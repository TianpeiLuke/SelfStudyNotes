## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Locally Metrizable Space

>[!important] Definition
>A space $X$ is **locally metrizable** if every point $x$ of $X$ has a *neighborhood* $U$ that is **metrizable** in the **subspace topology**.
