## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**:  Balanced  Set


>[!important] Definition
>A *set* $A$ is called **balanced** or **circled**, if for $x\in A$, and $|\lambda| \leq 1$, implies that $\lambda x \in C$
>$$
>x\in A, |\lambda| \le 1 \implies \lambda x \in A
>$$
