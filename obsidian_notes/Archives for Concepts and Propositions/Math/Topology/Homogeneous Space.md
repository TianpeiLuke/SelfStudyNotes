## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Homogeneous Space

>[!important] Definition
>A *topological space* $X$ is a **homogeneous space** if for every pair $x,y\in X$, there *exists* a *homemorphism* $f: X\to X$ such that $$f(x) = y.$$
