## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**:  Isolated Point


>[!important] Definition
>If $X$ is a space, a point $x$ of $X$ is said to be an **isolated point** of $X$ 
>
>if the *one-point set* $$\{x\}$$ is **open** in $X$.
