## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Leray-Schauder-Tychonoff Theorem on Fixed Point

>[!important] Theorem
>Let $C$ be a non-empty **compact convex** subset of a **locally convex space** $X$.
>
>Let $T: C\to C$ be a **continuous map**.
>
>Then $T$ has a **fixed point**.
