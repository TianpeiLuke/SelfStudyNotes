## Theorem

>[!important]
>**Name**: Smirnov Metrization Theorem



>[!important] Smirnov Metrization Theorem
>A space $X$ is **metrizable** *if and only if* $X$ is a **paracompact Hausdorff** space that is **locally metrizable**.
