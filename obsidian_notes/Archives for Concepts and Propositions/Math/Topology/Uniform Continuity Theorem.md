## Theorem

>[!important]
>**Name**: Uniform Continuity Theorem


>[!important] Uniform Continuity Theorem
> Let $f: X \rightarrow Y$ be a **continuous map** of the **compact** *metric space* $(X, d_X)$ to *the metric space* $(Y, d_Y)$. 
> 
> Then $f$ is **uniformly continuous**.
