## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Fixed Point of Map

>[!important] Definition
>Let $T: X \to X$ be a map on a set $X$. A point $x \in X$ for which 
>$$
>T(x) = x
>$$
>is called a **fixed point** of $T$.
