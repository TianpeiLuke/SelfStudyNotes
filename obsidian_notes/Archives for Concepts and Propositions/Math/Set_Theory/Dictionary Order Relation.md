## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Dictionary Order Relation

>[!important] Definition
>Suppose that $A$ and $B$ are two sets with *order relations*  $\prec_A$ and $\prec_B$ respectively. 
>
>Define an order relation $\prec$ on $A \times B$ by defining
>$$
> \begin{align*}
> a_1 \times b_1 < a_2 \times b_2
> \end{align*}
>$$ 
>if $a_1 <_A a_2$, *or* if $a_1 = a_2$ and $b_1 <_B b_2$. 
>
>It is called **the dictionary order relation** on $A \times B$.
