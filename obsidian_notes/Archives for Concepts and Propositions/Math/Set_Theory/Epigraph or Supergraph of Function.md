## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Epigraph of Function

>[!important] Definition
>Let $f: X \to \mathbb{R}$ be a function. 
>
>The set 
>$$
>\text{epi}(f) = \{ (x, \mu) \in X\times \mathbb{R}:  f(x) \le \mu\}
>$$
>is called the **epigraph (supergraph) of function** $f$ and is denoted $\text{epi}(f)$.
