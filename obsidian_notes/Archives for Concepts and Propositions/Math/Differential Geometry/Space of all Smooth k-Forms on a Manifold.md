## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Space of all Smooth $k$-form on a Manifold

>[!important] Definition
>We denote the *vector space* of **smooth $k$-forms** by
>$$
> \begin{align*}
> \Omega^{k}(M) &= \Gamma \left(\Lambda^k(T^{*}M)\right).
> \end{align*}
>$$
