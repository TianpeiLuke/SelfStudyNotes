## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**:  Young Inequality

>[!important] Young Inequality
>Let $p ,q \in [1, +\infty]$ with $$\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{q} = 1.$$
>
>Then for any $a, b \ge 0$, 
>$$
>\begin{align*}
> a\,b \le \frac{a^p}{p} + \frac{b^q}{q}.   
>\end{align*}
>$$
>where the equality is achieved when $$a^p = b^q.$$
