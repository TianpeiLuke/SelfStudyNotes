## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Left Coset and Right Coset of Group


>[!important] Definition
>For $H\subset G$ as the *subgroup* of $G$, define the **left coset** as 
>$$xH = \set{x\cdot h: h\in H}.$$ 
>Similarly, define the **right coset** as 
>$$Hx = \set{h\cdot x: h\in H}.$$
