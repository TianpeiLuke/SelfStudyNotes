## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Ring with Identity

>[!important] Definition
>The *ring* $R$ is said to **have an identity** (or **contain a $1$**) if there exists an element $1_{R}\in R$ such that for all $a\in R$,
>$$
>1_{R} \times a = a \times 1_{R} = a.
>$$
