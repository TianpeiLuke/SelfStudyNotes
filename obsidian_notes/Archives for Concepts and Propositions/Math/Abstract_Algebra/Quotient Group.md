## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Quotient Group


>[!important] Definition
>The collection of *left cosets* defines a **quotient group** $$G/H = \set{ xH\, |\, x\in G}$$ with *the group operation* $$xH \cdot yH = (x\cdot y) H.$$
