## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Image of Group Homomorphism

>[!important] Definition
>Given two *groups*, $(G,∗)$ and $(H,\cdot)$, and $f: (G,∗) \to (H,\cdot)$, is a *group homomorphism.*
>
>The **image** of $f$ is defined as the set
>$$
>\text{im}(f) = \{ f(x) \in H: x \in G \}
>$$
>
