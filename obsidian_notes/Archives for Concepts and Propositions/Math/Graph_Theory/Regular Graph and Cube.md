## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: k-Regular Graph

![[Degree of Vertex#^75a6dd]]


>[!important] Definition
>If all the vertices of $G$ have the *same degree* $k$, then $G$ is **$k$-regular**, or simply **regular**.

>[!important] Definition
>A $3$-regular graph is called a **cube** or a **cubic graph**.
