## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Spanning Subgraph

![[Induced Subgraph#^ab6014]]

>[!important] Definition
>Let $G' = (V', E')$ and $G = (V, E)$.
>
>The graph $G' \subseteq G$ is a **spanning subgraph** *of* $G$ if
>- $V'$ *spans all* of $G$, i.e. $$V' = V.$$
