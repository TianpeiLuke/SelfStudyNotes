## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Maximum Variation


>[!important] Defintion
>Define the **maximum variation** between $\{f_n\}_{n}$ and $f$ as $$\sup_{x\in X}|f_{n}(x)- f(x)|.$$ 


>[!info]
>Note that 
>$$
> \begin{align*}
>  \sup\limits_{x\in X}|f_{n}(x)- f(x)| &\ge \sup_{x\in X\setminus F, \mu(F) =0}|f_{n}(x)- f(x)|.
> \end{align*}
> $$
