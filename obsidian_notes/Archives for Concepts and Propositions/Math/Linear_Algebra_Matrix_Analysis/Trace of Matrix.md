## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Trace of Matrix


>[!important] Definition
>Let $A = [a_{i,j}] \in M_{n}$ be a $n\times n$ matrix.
>
>The **trace** of matrix $A$ is the *sum of diagonal entries* of $A$ $$\text{tr}(A) = \sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{i,i}.$$
