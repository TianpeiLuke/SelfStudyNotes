## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Commutative Ring

>[!important] Definition
>The *ring* $R$ is **commutative** (or **contain a $1$**) if the *multiplication operation* is commutative. 
>
>That is, for all $a, b \in R$,
>$$
>a \times b = b \times a.
>$$
