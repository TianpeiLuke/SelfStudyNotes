## Concept Definition

>[!important]
>**Name**: Project Planning Schedule

>[!important] Definition
>The **schedule** is basically a *timeline* of the project. 
>- It includes 
>	- the start date, 
>	- the end date, 
>	- and the dates for things that will happen in between.
