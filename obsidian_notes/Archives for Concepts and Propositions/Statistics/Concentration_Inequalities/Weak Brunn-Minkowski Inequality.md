>[!important] Weak Brunn-Minkowski Inequality
>Let $A, B \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ be *non-empty* **compact sets**. 
>
>Then for all $\lambda \in [0, 1]$,
>$$
> \begin{align}
> \text{Vol}\left(\lambda A + (1- \lambda) B \right) &\ge \text{Vol}(A)^{\lambda}\text{Vol}(B)^{1- \lambda}. 
> \end{align}
> $$
