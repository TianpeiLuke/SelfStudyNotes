# Abuse Type

In this work, the main target is ***the MFN Delivered-Not-Received (DNR) Abuse.*** A *DNR* case occurs when the customer claimed that the item was not received even though it was reported as *Delivered* according to the Shipment Tracking information. According to Amazon refund and return policy, Amazon would ship another item free-of-charge to the customer. A *DNR abuse* happens when customer lied to Amazon to obtain additional item for free. The DNR sector alone contributes to the majority of concession related loss for Amazon.
