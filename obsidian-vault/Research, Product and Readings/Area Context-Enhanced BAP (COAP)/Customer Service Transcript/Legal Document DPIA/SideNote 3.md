- **Note**: Information security risk is managed by teams such as InfoSec, Anvil, TPS, etc. While there is overlap, we are looking for measures that have been put in place to deal with privacy risk specifically, including, e.g., data minimization and access controls.

- Questions to ask yourself:

	- Why is the data retention period appropriate?
	  
	- What technical and organizational measures will Amazon implement to protect the data (minimization, de-identification, aggregation)?
	  
	- Has this Tool been registered with InfoSec/Anvil/TPS?
	  
	- Can customers request access to, rectification, or deletion of the information held by Amazon in this Tool?
