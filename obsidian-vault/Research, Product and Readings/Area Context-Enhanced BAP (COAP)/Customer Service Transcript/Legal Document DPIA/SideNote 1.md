Questions to ask yourself: 

- **Why is the Tool necessary to process the data?**

- **Does Amazon obtain consent (express or implied) from data subjects to collect, process and store the data?**
  
- **Is further processing compatible with the original purpose of collection?**
  
- **Can data subjects access the Amazon Privacy Notice and obtain all relevant information on how their personal data is collected, processed and stored by Amazon?**
  
- **Is processing reasonable and fair to customers?**
  
- **Are we applying data minimization principles (consider downstream requirements) and access controls to processing?**

- **Are we ensuring data quality and accuracy?**

-  **Are there alternatives to collection, processing, or storing the data in the Tool? What are potential negative consequences if not implemented? Is there no less intrusive way? Why don't alternatives work?**
  
- **Why won’t using de-identified or aggregated data work?**
