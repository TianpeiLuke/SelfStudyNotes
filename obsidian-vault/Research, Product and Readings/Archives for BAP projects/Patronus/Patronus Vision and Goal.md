>[!quote]
> ## PQ team vision: 
> 
> - For Amazon to be where every customer has confidence that all the products they see and purchase on Amazon stores are authentic and will be delivered in the condition and with the functionality they expect.
> 
> ## Goal: 
> - Our goal is to offer Patronus as a shared service to maximize customer impact by reusing existing capabilities and driving feature development that continuously reduces the resource requirements, across science and engineering, to launch new programs based on insights gained from customer feedback.
> 
> 
> To deliver on our vision to make Patronus a one-stop shop for detecting risk by listening to customers we will focus on 3 key areas, 
> 
> - **Building self-service capabilities** to allow clients to develop, configure and deploy detection mechanisms (including ML models, RMP rules) tailored to their specific business objectives using *already available feedback sources on Patronus with minimal support* from engineers and scientists, 
> - **Streamline onboarding** to allow clients to onboard new data sources and configure their own workflows to detect objects tailored to their specific business requirements, and 
> - **Scalability** enabling clients to run multiple detection mechanisms (heuristic rules, ML models built by self-service or customized ML models built by client teams) in parallel and process millions of customer feedback efficiently. In this document, we present our vision for Patronus (or detection system) as an extensible, scalable and self-service solution which will enable teams across Amazon to build and deploy customized Natural Language Processing (NLP) solutions to detect, classify, summarize or extract insights from customer communications.
>
