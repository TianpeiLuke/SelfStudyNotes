The diversification of action taking systems across SPS over the years has led to a suboptimal customer experience where buyers and selling partners are communicated to and enforced upon in varied, duplicative, and inconsistent ways. This fragmentation of enforcement systems results in: 
1. Enforcements being implemented redundantly (operationally expensive to maintain), 
2. Overly harsh enforcements on customers,
3. Inconsistent communication of enforcements to customers and partners,
4. Over-dependence on human judgement to apply or revoke enforcements, and
5. Lack of transparency into risk policies across program silos.


To address these pain points, the SPS Actions Program’s vision is to empower all risk programs to balance customer experience & risk mitigation objectives more quickly and frugally via **the Action Taking System (ATS)** as a unified and policy-driven enforcement pipeline that enables the buyer, seller, and brand businesses to protect our stores.
