Context-based Buyer Abuse Prevention (COAP) system is a proposed online evaluation system that identifies buyer abuse risk based on customer's transaction information as well as the context information from customer's contact history. The goal of COAP is to listen to the voice of customers and to enhance the precision of our existing decision workflow with customer contacts such as Customer Service Contacts. COAP provides event summarization, feature extraction and risk classification support to human investigators, who then make the final decisions on whether or not a given customer/order is violating Amazon's policy. Overall, our work can be seen as an extension of the Voice of Customer (VoC) in the domain of buyer abuse evaluation.
