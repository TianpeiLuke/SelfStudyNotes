>[!comment]
>* List which customers’ data is processed by the Tool.

The customer data to be processed by COAP are the Customer Service Contact Transcripts (CSC), which includes the chat transcript, phone transcript and email transcript between buyer and CS agents. A complete list of variables available can be found in wiki from CS Neuron team. [Link](https://w.amazon.com/bin/view/CS_Neuron_and_Wit/Neuron/Junction/Data) to the wiki. 

Besides CSC, COAP also consumes customer's order history and refund/return history on Amazon as well as customer's enforcement history by Amazon. These are all customer-level aggregated variables.
