## Seller CS team

> [!quote]
> Seller Customer is a team based in Detroit. Our vision is to ensure that the Amazon Marketplace provides the world’s best Buyer experience. To achieve this vision, we need to have *reduce the number and duration of Buyer contacts to Selling Partners.* We see all *Selling Partner contacts as defects*. We find that every additional message in an interaction reduces Buyer satisfaction 405.94 bps and increases the likelihood of reporting the Interaction by 311.02 bps. This is highlighted by an 889.65 bps reduction in satisfaction and 999.13 bps increase in response rate after 4 messages. The main opportunity we pursue is to shift our architecture to capture each customer interaction, proactive or reactive, as a case. Case creation will be available globally across all points of interaction (Desktop, Mobile, API, Alexa). Cases engage with Buyers at the appropriate level for the interaction. Cases help Selling Partners prioritize customer service inquiries based on severity of the CS issue. Cases provide critical context to customer service interactions – we’ll combine that context with the interaction length and result to improve the Buyer experience internally and externally.

## Team Mission

>[!quote]
We prioritize investments in three key themes 
> 1) eliminating Selling Partner contacts, 
> 2) increasing first contact resolution rate, and 
> 3) reducing the number of messages required to resolve a Selling Partner contact. 
> 
> We see all Selling Partner contacts as defects.

>[!quote]
>Our primary customers are *Buyers*. We provide Selling Partners with the tools necessary to give Buyers the best experience possible. When there is ambiguity in the capabilities of our tools to provide bar raising experiences, we will issue policies to inform Selling Partners of our expectations. As a highly instrumented conduit for the customer experience we make our data available to other teams and when our data raises deficiencies in another team’s tools, we will seek them out to resolve the issue.

## Tenets (unless you know better ones…)

- Buyer & Seller trust is paramount. We strive to build Buyer and Seller trust, even if it means adopting unpopular policies. 
- Anonymous communications. We will protect Seller and Buyer privacy without exception, even if it results in short-term loss. 
- Buyer satisfaction is more important than Seller experience. We disproportionately invest in features that improve Buyer satisfaction over improving Seller experience.
- Order related communications. Buyers and Sellers communicate when it enables or facilitates order completion. Any communication that doesn’t fit these criteria is an exception and should be on a deprecation path.
- Messaging is not an acceptable substitute for automating order workflows. We do not require Selling Partners or Buyers to use messaging to complete a core order workflow.
- Open, available platform. BSM platform is open and available to all client teams who want to mine BSM data and build programmatic solutions at scale.
- Selling Partners don’t initiate communicate with Buyers about products they don’t already own.
- Customer service can only be improved if it can be measured. We provide analytics that let Selling Partners make data-based decisions.
- Resolving a Buyer’s issue before engaging a Selling Partner is the best Customer Service.
- We use our data classification capability to act as canaries for Customer Service issues across Selling Partner Services.
