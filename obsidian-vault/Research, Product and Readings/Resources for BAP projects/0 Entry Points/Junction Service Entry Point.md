## Introduction

>[!info] **What is Junction?**
> 
> Junction is a service which aggregates and unifies training data for Customer Service Machine Learning models. We provide clean data, unified between offline and online sources, which allows ML scientists to train robust ML models of all types.

## Owner

Junction service is owned by **CS Neuron team**. See [the team internal wiki](https://w.amazon.com/bin/view/CS_Neuron_and_Wit/Neuron/)

>[!info] 
>**CS Neuron** is a software engineering team in the Customer Engagement Technologies org. We build services and tools that use, and enable other teams to use, Machine Learning to solve customer problems.
