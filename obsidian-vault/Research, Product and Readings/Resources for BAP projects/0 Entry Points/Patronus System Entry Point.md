## Introduction

>[!info] **What is Patronus?**

- currently listens to 6 sources of customer feedback to identify Defects including: Customer Service Contacts, emails between buyers and sellers (BBC), Product Reviews, Seller Feedback and messages provided during Returns and Claims.

- Augment them with information such as the complaint text, ASIN, Order, Seller Provenance related attributes, model scores that help enforcement decision making.
